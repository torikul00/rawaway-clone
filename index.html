About Us

Rawaway unites a carefully curated global business community specializing in metal trading, comprising experts in international trade, financial structuring, legal professionals, government representatives, and leading CEOs.

Together, we share a common vision: to deliver innovative solutions and enable the seamless exchange of metals and minerals across the globe. Our collaborative approach ensures that we effectively navigate the complexities of the market, facilitating reliable transactions and sustainable partnerships in the metal trading industry.

Solutions

We excel at orchestrating complex supply chains to transport commodities from their source of production to their final destination. By sourcing raw materials and goods directly from our extensive global network of suppliers, we deliver them to our partners across the world. This process includes shipping by sea, rail, and road, as well as the storage, processing, and timely delivery of goods, all while ensuring the highest standards of quality and meeting the precise specifications of our international clients.

We empower our clients, leaders in steel production, to reach their targets in terms of: Efficiency: Their EAFs use electrical energy, providing flexibility and faster production cycles compared to traditional blast furnaces. Sustainability: By recycling scrap steel, they contribute to reducing carbon emissions and minimizing the use of raw materials like iron ore. Innovation: We support them to stay ahead of industry trends and their implementation of cutting-edge technology, ensuring that their steel products meet the highest standards.

Scraps Metals

Heavy Melting Steel (HMS): This includes large, heavy pieces of scrap steel, such as beams, plates, and structures. HMS is often the primary source of scrap for EAFs.

Shredded Scrap: This is scrap metal that has been shredded into smaller pieces, making it easier to melt and process in an EAF. Shredded scrap typically includes mixed metals from vehicles and appliances

Obsolete Scrap: This category encompasses discarded products like old machinery, vehicles, appliances, and construction debris. It can vary in composition but is typically a mix of ferrous and non-ferrous metals.
